Hi! My name’s Matty Freeman.

I’m currently pursuing a career in web design and feel very fortunate to have just finished interning for a software company called Friendly Manager. I worked with a small team, helping them problem solve and develop design solutions by conducting research, creating wireframes, building user flows, MVPs and more for the developer to then build on. Along with interning at Friendly Manager I also worked for them part time as the user support person. My UX design skills grew a lot through the role and I believe I developed a greater sense of empathy for the user by seeing and hearing from the their perspective. Not only have I worked for Friendly Manager but I’m also currently enrolled with Bloc, an online coding bootcamp, incorporating 1-on-1 mentorship. Bloc’s equipping me with essential design skills and helping me build an extremely cool portfolio. I’m very excited to work in an industry I love and am always looking to learn more and grow my skills to the next level.

I really want to hear from you so don't hesitate to get in touch even to just tell some stories or get a coffee.
